About Me

Amena Ahmed received her bachelor's in Elementary Education from Northeastern Illinois University. She taught in various classrooms for over five years, including special education, middle school literature, and primary grades. She turned to computer science and web development to channel her inner creative side. She is currenlty pursuing web development as a student at Northwestern University's Coding Bootcamp and aspires to study computer science and work to create a positive impact in education, health care, and other humanitarian causes. She currently lives in Skokie with her family. In her spare time she loves to hike, go camping, and explore nature.
